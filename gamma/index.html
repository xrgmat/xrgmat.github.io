Seminar GAMMA:
Geometry and Applications. Modern Mathematical Approaches

The Geometry, Applications, an Modern Mathematical Approaches (Gamma) Seminar is an online seminar aimed at discussing basic and advanced research topics conducted by the common Gamma Polish-Spanish geometric group gathering people from the University of Warsaw, the International University of La Rioja, the Polytechnic University of Madrid, ICMAT, and the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

■ Talks

Abstract

This talk is aimed at reviewing the basic notions on symplectic geometry, momentum maps, and the Marsden-Weinstein reduction theory.
