Seminar GAMMA:
Geometry and Applications. Modern Mathematical Approaches

The Geometry, Applications, an Modern Mathematical Approaches (Gamma) Seminar is an online seminar aimed at discussing basic and advanced research topics conducted by the common Gamma Polish-Spanish geometric group gathering people from the University of Warsaw, the International University of La Rioja, the Polytechnic University of Madrid, ICMAT, and the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

■ Talks

Abstract

This talk, divided into two parts, is mostly aimed at explaining fundamental facts on symplectic geometry, the Marsden-Weinstein-Meyer reduction theorem, and their applications in Physics. I will try to provide some not very well-known facts to make to talk more interesting to those knowing the theory, while keeping it simple for beginners. Facts about modern research problems concerning the Marsden-Weinstein Meyer theorem will be detailed.
