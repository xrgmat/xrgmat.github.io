Seminar GAMMA:
Geometry and Applications. Modern Mathematical Approaches

The Geometry and Applications: Modern Mathematical Approaches (Gamma) Seminar is a weekly online seminar aimed at discussing basic and advanced research topics conducted by the Polish-Spanish geometric group gathering people from the University of Warsaw, the International University of La Rioja, the Polytechnic University of Madrid, ICMAT, and the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

e-mail: gamma.seminar(at)gmail.com

■ Conferences

Abstract

This talk, divided into three parts, is mostly aimed at explaining fundamental facts on symplectic geometry, the Marsden-Weinstein-Meyer reduction theorem, and their applications in Physics. I will try to provide some not very well-known facts to make to talk more interesting to those knowing the theory, while keeping it simple for beginners. Facts about modern research problems concerning the Marsden-Weinstein-Meyer theorem will be detailed.
