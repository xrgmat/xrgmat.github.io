How to reach us

The Faculty of Physics of the University of Warsaw is in Ludwika Pasteura 5, 02-093 Warsaw, Poland. The conference will take place in Room 1.03, in the first floor.

Restaurants

The following restaurants are close the Faculty of Physics:

  • Cafeteria of the Faculty of Biology: Cheap and good local food.
  • Jeff's: American food.
  • Kuchnia Za Ścianą: Polish food.

The following restaurants are around the city center and serve local food:

  • U Szwejka. This restaurant is located in Plac Konstytucji, right below the MDM Hotel.
  • Zapiecek. There are many Zapiecek restaurants in Warsaw. This one is located next to the old town. Their speciality are pierogi.
  • Syrena Irena. This restaurant is placed next to the statue of Copernicus, in Nowy Świat.
  • Lokal Vegan Bistro. A vegan restaurant close to the Palace of Culture and Science.
